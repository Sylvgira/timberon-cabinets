Bathroom cabinetry has to work harder than any other room in the house. Humidity, temperature swings, and constant use mean every joint, coating, and seal matters.

We build bathroom vanities, mirror cabinets, and storage units that handle moisture without compromise. All carcases use waterproof materials, all edges are sealed, and every surface is specified for the environment it will live in.

## What is included

- Custom vanity units in any size or configuration
- Mirror cabinets with integrated lighting and power
- Linen storage and tallboys
- Solid surface, stone, or timber vanity tops
- Soft-close drawers and doors throughout
- Waterproof substrate and sealed joinery
- Tapware cut-outs and splashback coordination

## What makes our bathroom cabinetry different

- Moisture-resistant MDF or marine ply carcases as standard
- All cut edges sealed before assembly
- Drawer bases epoxy-coated for water resistance
- Hardware rated for high-humidity environments

Homeowners, renovators, and builders who want bathroom cabinetry that still looks new five years in.
