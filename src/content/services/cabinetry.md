Not every project fits neatly into a room category. Some of the best cabinetry we build sits in the spaces between — a study nook under the stairs, a bar cabinet in the dining room, a mudroom with cubbies for four kids, a display wall for a book collector.

We treat every piece of joinery as a custom build. One-off sizes, unusual materials, tight access — these are not complications, they are the brief.

## What is included

- Home office cabinetry and desk joinery
- Bar cabinets and wine storage
- Entertainment units and media walls
- Mudrooms, hall tables, and entry joinery
- Display shelving and bookcases
- Custom storage for unusual spaces
- Integrated lighting, power, and cable management
- Solid timber, veneer, polyurethane, or laminate finishes

## What to expect

- On-site measure and space analysis
- CAD drawings and material samples
- Off-site construction in our Thomastown workshop
- Professional installation and finish

## Best fit

Homeowners with a specific vision or an awkward space that standard furniture cannot solve.
