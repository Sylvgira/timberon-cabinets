The kitchen is the most-used room in the house. Every drawer, every door, every work surface should earn its place.

We design and build kitchen cabinetry that fits your space exactly — not a showroom standard rearranged to suit a floor plan. Whether you are renovating a period home in Northcote or building new in Wollert, we work with your architect, builder, or interior designer to deliver cabinetry that looks like it grew there.

## What is included

- Full design consultation and on-site measuring
- Custom carcasses in moisture-resistant MDF or plywood
- Solid timber, polyurethane, or laminate door fronts
- Soft-close runners and hinges throughout
- Integrated panel-ready appliance fronts
- Benchtop templating and installation coordination
- Drawer organisers, waste bins, and custom inserts

## Materials we work with

- European oak, American walnut, blackbutt, and Victorian ash
- High-gloss and matte polyurethane in any colour
- Laminate and compact laminate for high-use areas
- Natural stone, engineered stone, and timber benchtops

## Best fit

This service suits homeowners, builders, and designers who want kitchen cabinetry that is built to last — not flat-pack boards screwed together on site.
