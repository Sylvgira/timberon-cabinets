A well-designed laundry makes weekly chores faster and less frustrating. We specialise in laundry cabinetry that turns a forgotten room into one of the most functional spaces in the house.

From compact European-style laundries in inner-city apartments to full utility rooms in new builds, we plan every millimetre for storage and workflow.

## What is included

- Base and overhead cabinets in any configuration
- Pull-out hampers and sorting bins
- Fold-down ironing stations
- Broom and vacuum cupboard storage
- Deep drawers for detergents and supplies
- Drying rack integrated cabinetry
- Splashback and benchtop coordination
- Moisture-resistant construction throughout

## Design considerations

- Work triangle: basket → washer → folding surface → drawer
- Bench height matched to the user
- Ventilation allowances for dryers
- Power point and plumbing access planned into cabinetry

## Best fit

Homeowners and renovators who want a laundry that works as hard as they do.
